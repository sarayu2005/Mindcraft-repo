CLASSIFIED ARCHIVES: DATA ANALYST

WHAT IS IT?
Data Analysis is the systematic process of inspecting, cleansing, transforming, and modeling data to discover useful information, inform conclusions, and support decision-making.
WHO DOES IT?
Data Analysts: Detectives of the digital age. They bridge the gap between raw, chaotic data streams and strategic business leaders who need answers.
HOW IS IT USED?
Using Python libraries as the primary toolkit:
  • Pandas: For data manipulation (DataFrames).
  • NumPy: For numerical calculations (Arrays).
  • Matplotlib: For visualizing trends (Plots).
WHY IS IT CRITICAL?
Without analysis, data is just noise. It helps organizations:
- Predict future market trends.
- Optimize operational efficiency.
- Eliminate guesswork in strategy.
WHERE & WHEN?
Used globally in Finance, Healthcare, Tech, and Marketing. Real-time analytics happens 24/7 (e.g., fraud detection), while batch analytics happens for quarterly reports.
